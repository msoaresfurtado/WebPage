Honors students: Interested in summer research? The L&S Honors Program offers opportunities to gain funded, hands-on summer experience through the Honors Summer Research Apprenticeship program.

I've thoroughly enjoyed mentoring students through this program in the past—feel free to reach out if you'd like to brainstorm a summer project together.

Applying for the NASA Hubble Fellowship Program or the 51 Peg b Fellowship? UW-Madison is a fantastic place to bring these prestigious awards. Our department spans stellar astrophysics, exoplanet science, cosmology, and multi-messenger astronomy, with active interdisciplinary collaborations through the Wisconsin Center for Origins Research (WiCOR). We have access to world-class facilities, including SALT, WIYN, and NOEMA. Madison itself consistently ranks among the most livable cities in the U.S., with a thriving community, excellent public transit, and a cost of living that lets you actually enjoy your postdoc years. We're committed to supporting fellows in building independent research programs, not just plugging into existing ones!

If you're considering applying, feel free to reach out to discuss potential synergies or hosting opportunities.
