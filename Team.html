Honors students: Interested in summer research? The L&S Honors Program offers opportunities to gain funded, hands-on summer experience through the Honors Summer Research Apprenticeship program.

I've thoroughly enjoyed mentoring students through this program in the past—feel free to reach out if you'd like to brainstorm a summer project together.

Applying for the NASA Hubble Fellowship Program or the 51 Peg b Fellowship? UW–Madison is a fantastic place to bring these prestigious awards. UW–Madison, in partnership with the interdisciplinary WiCOR initiative, offers an exceptional environment to advance the pioneering research supported by these prestigious fellowships.

If you're considering applying, feel free to reach out to discuss potential synergies or hosting opportunities.
